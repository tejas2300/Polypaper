PE Coated Paper

Minimum Order Quantity: 200 KgProduct Description:

Using the right materials for packaging plays a major role in ensuring that the customers receive goods with the best possible presentation. PE Coated Paper is layered with polyethylene, providing a very strong moisture and grease barrier.

Applications/ Features:
  • RK PE Coated Paper is excellent for providing strong moisture and grease barrier.
  • Available in 70 as well as 120 GSM roll form
  • Available in different types such as Maplitho PE Coated paper, Chromo PE Coated paper, etc.
  • Extremely durable, allowing it to be used for wide range of application process
  • PE Coated paper can be used for hot purposes as it won't melt and affect your product
  • Specially designed for plate making
